About Us

Kentucky Soccer Officials Association has served the Louisville community since 1983. Our goal is to create a friendly, safe environment for our referees to grow on and off the field. We are always looking for new members and welcome anyone interested in growing their knowledge of the game.

Frequently Asked Questions

How old do I have to be to become a referee? US Soccer recommends a person be at least 12 years of age.
How much do the games pay? On average, games pay about $25 per game. Often times, the pay is much higher.
How early should I arrive before a game? United States Soccer Federation mandates a minimum arrival time of 30 minutes prior to the match.
